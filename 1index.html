Kohei Uchimura: Introduction

Kohei Uchimura is a Japanese gymnast who is considered one of the greatest in the history of the sport. Born on January 3, 1989, in Kitakyushu, Fukuoka, Japan, he stands at 162 cm and became known worldwide for his precision, artistry, and dominance in mens artistic gymnastics. Competing in four Olympic Games from 2008 to 2020, he earned seven Olympic medals, including three golds, and ten World Championship golds. His reputation as “King Kohei” reflects his ability to combine technical difficulty with elegance, making him nearly unbeatable in the all aroung category for almost a decade.
