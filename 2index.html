Kohei Uchimura: Upbringing

Uchimuras journey began in a family deeply connected to gymnastics. His parents were both gymnasts, and his mother later became a coach. He started training at a very young age, often spending long hours in the gym refining his skills. From the beginning, he focused on discipline and thoughtful practice rather than relying solely on natural talent. This approach, valuing quality of execution over sheer difficulty, was instilled during his childhood and became the foundation of his career.
